### Phase 5 (Selection, Award, Execution) - PENDING

**Strative AI Help Desk Prototype OTA Award:**
- **Target Contract Value:** ~$471,000
- **Scope:** AI-Enabled Help Desk for PCTE (not PCTE-in-a-Box)
- **Approval authority:** Commanding Officer / Director / Senior Procurement Executive

**Prototype Duration:**
- Expected: 6-12 months
- Milestone-based payment structure

**Payment Structure:**
- Milestone-based (not T&M)
- Two types:
  1. Fixed Milestone Payments (predetermined amounts upon milestone achievement)
  2. Expenditure-Based Milestone Payments (milestone value as maximum, adjusted based on actual expenditures)
- Certificate of Completion required for each milestone to trigger payment

## Recommended Phase 5 Negotiation Talking Points

### 1. Contract Value & Structure
- **Ask:** What is the anticipated prototype OTA value range?
- **Rationale:** Align resource planning with funding level
- **Positioning:** Emphasize technical depth and integration complexity warrant appropriate funding

### 2. Milestone Definition
- **Ask:** Will CFIC provide sample milestone structure or template?
- **Rationale:** Ensure clarity and achievability
- **Positioning:** Propose aggressive but realistic milestones aligned with PEO STRI's 6-month rapid prototyping model

### 3. Follow-On Production Pathway
- **Ask:** Will the prototype OTA explicitly include provisions for non-competitive follow-on production?
- **Rationale:** Statutory requirement under 10 U.S.C. ยง4022(f)
- **Positioning:** Emphasize commitment to PCTE integration and long-term partnership

### 4. Payment Terms & Schedule
- **Ask:** Fixed milestone or expenditure-based milestone payments?
- **Rationale:** Plan cash flow and financial management
- **Positioning:** Demonstrate capability to track and manage milestone-based payments rigorously

### 5. Integration & DevSecOps Support
- **Ask:** What level of PCTE DevSecOps support will be provided?
- **Rationale:** Understand integration resource requirements
- **Positioning:** Emphasize readiness to integrate with PCTE enterprise architecture
